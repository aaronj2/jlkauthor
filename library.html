One Step at a Time

This method series is a classic new approach for teaching violin, viola, cello, or double bass, in the private studio. The fundamentals are presented in a logical, sequential, and easy to understand way. There is a piano score for each of the five books.

Easy as Do-Re-Mi

If you're looking for an effective book for teaching in the school system, check this out.


Complete in three books, this is an innovative approach to teaching strings in the classroom of any age. It is based on the established ONE STEP method, where fundamentals are introduced, one at a time, on uncluttered pages, with an emphasis on finger patterns and tetrachords.
